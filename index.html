01陌生社交场NetworkingNetworking

反差钩子 Contrast Hook · ~30s

你好，我叫 YC。跟你老实说，很多人第一次听我做什么，心里都会「哦」一声——「又一个拉人做生意的」。我懂那个反应，我以前也这样看这一行。

后来我才发现，我真正在做的，不是卖东西给谁，而是帮一个普通人，先把身体顾好，再把自己带起来，最后把一群人带起来。

所以现在别人问我做什么，我会说：我是做「领导力生态」的——建健康、建成长、建领袖。今天认识你，我不急着谈什么，先交个朋友。

Hi, I'm YC. Let me be upfront with you — when most people hear what I do, there's this little pause. "Ah. Another one of those MLM guys." I get it. I used to see this industry exactly the same way.

But here's what I actually do. I help ordinary people get their health sorted first, then grow into who they can be, and then lead others to do the same.

So these days when people ask, I tell them: I build leadership ecosystems — health, growth, leaders, in that order. Anyway, no agenda today. Let's just get to know each other first.

02台上培训开场On StageStage

反差钩子 + 化繁为简 · ~30s

各位早上好，我是 YC。开场我先替在座一部分人，把心里那句话讲出来：「这个人是不是又要推销什么了？」

没关系，这个印象我扛得住。因为我今天站在这里，不是要卖你任何东西，我是来陪你练一件事——怎么一开口，就有影响力。

我做的事说穿了就一件：建立人。先建健康，再建成长，然后建领袖。接下来这段时间，我只教你一个核心、三个步骤，你今晚回去就能用。

Good morning, everyone. I'm YC. Before anything else, let me say out loud what some of you are already thinking: "Here comes the pitch."

It's okay — I can carry that one. Because I'm not standing here to sell you anything. I'm here to train one thing with you: how to open your mouth and actually be heard.

Strip away everything, and what I do is one thing — building people. Health first, then growth, then leaders. For the next while, I'll give you one core idea and three steps. You can use them tonight.

03一对一咖啡Over CoffeeCasual

反差钩子 + 礼物思维 · ~30s

先谢谢你抽空出来喝这杯。我先自首一下——我知道我这行，很多人一听就防备，觉得「他是不是要拉我」。你有这个念头，很正常。

但我今天真的没这个打算。我这些年做的，其实是陪人成长：身体先照顾好，人先立起来，能带人了，路自然就宽了。

所以我不谈生意，我想先听你的故事。等一下你讲，你现在最想突破的是哪一块，我看看我手上有没有对你有用的东西，有就送你。

First — thanks for making time for this coffee. And let me just confess something up front: I know the moment people hear my line of work, the guard goes up. "Is he going to recruit me?" Totally fair. I'd think the same.

But honestly, that's not today's plan. What I've really been doing all these years is walking with people while they grow — get the health in order, get the person standing strong, and once you can lead others, the road opens up on its own.

So, no business talk. I'd rather hear your story first. Tell me — what's the one thing you're trying to break through right now? If I've got something useful for that, it's yours.

鼓励 + 纠偏谈话Praise + Course-Correct

甜咸比 4:1 · 情境：领袖跟进很弱Scene: a leader whose follow-up is weak

肯定① 我先讲真心话，你带人的那股热，是真的。新人到你手上，都愿意开口，这不是每个领袖都做得到的。

肯定② 你上次那场分享，我在后面看，你眼睛是发光的，大家被你点起来了。

肯定③ 而且你肯学，我讲一次你就抓住，这是我最放心你的地方。

肯定④ 所以我从来不担心你「会不会」，我知道你会。

建议 只有一件小事想提醒你——你把人点亮了，就别让那团火隔了三天才回。今天起，我们一起立个规矩：当天认识的，48 小时内一定回一句。你的开场那么强，就差这一口气把它收进来。

收尾 这不是你不行，这是你只差最后一哩路。我陪你走。

Affirm 1 Let me say this straight — the fire you bring when you're with people? That's real. New people open up around you, and not every leader can do that.

Affirm 2 That sharing you did last week — I was watching from the back. Your eyes were lit up, and the whole room caught it from you.

Affirm 3 And you're teachable. I say something once, you've got it. That's the thing I trust most about you.

Affirm 4 So I've never once worried about whether you can do this. I know you can.

Suggest Just one small thing. You light people up — so don't let that fire sit for three days before you circle back. From today, one rule, you and me together: anyone you meet, one message within 48 hours. Your opening game is that strong. This is just the last touch that brings it home.

Closer This is not about ability. You're one mile from done — and I'll walk that mile with you.

培训开场 90 秒90-Second Training Opener

相似性联结 + 一个核心 Common Ground + One Core

各位晚上好。开始之前，我想先跟你们讲一件我自己的事。

很多人以为我天生会讲话。其实不是。我第一次站上台，手是抖的，讲三句话就忘词，台下有人低头看手机，我到现在都记得那个画面。我跟你们里面很多人一样，不是不想开口，是一开口就慌。

后来我才明白——影响力不是天生的口才，是练出来的胆量。你现在坐在这里，就已经比昨天的自己往前一步了。

今晚我们只做好一件事：让你敢开口，而且一开口，别人愿意听。就这一个核心。等一下三个步骤，你跟着练，练完今天，你回去就敢讲。

Good evening, everyone. Before we start, let me tell you something about myself.

People assume I was born knowing how to speak. I wasn't. The first time I stood on a stage, my hands were shaking. Three sentences in, my mind went blank. Someone in the crowd was looking down at his phone — I can still see that picture today. I'm the same as many of you in this room: it's not that we don't want to speak. It's that the moment we open our mouth, we panic.

It took me years to learn this: influence is not a gift of the gab. It's courage, trained. And the fact that you're sitting here tonight already puts you one step ahead of who you were yesterday.

Tonight we do one thing only: get you to speak up — and speak so that people want to listen. That's the core. Three steps coming up. Practise them with me, and you'll walk out of here ready to talk.

面对家人／朋友的质疑When Family Doubts You

认可 → 观点 → 价值对齐 · 「你怎么还在做这个？」Acknowledge → Position → Align · "Why are you still doing this?"

认可 我知道你这样问，是因为你在乎我，怕我走弯路。这份心我收到了，谢谢你一直没放弃劝我。

观点 但我想让你看看我真正在做的是什么。我不是在「卖」什么，我是在陪一群人把生活重新建起来——先顾健康，再练成长，慢慢有人从被我带，变成能带别人。

价值对齐 我们其实要的是同一样东西——你希望我踏实、稳、活得有意义。我做这件事，正是因为它让我每天都在帮到人，也在成为我想成为的那种人。你要的那个「好」，我正走在路上。

收尾 你不用马上认同我。只求你别急着替我担心，给我一点时间，用结果让你看到。

Acknowledge I know why you're asking me this. You care about me, and you don't want to watch me waste my years. I hear that — and thank you for never giving up on me.

Position But let me show you what I'm actually doing. I'm not "selling" anything to anyone. I walk with people while they rebuild their lives — health first, then growth, and slowly some of them go from being led by me to leading others themselves.

Align You and I want the same thing, you know. You want me steady, grounded, living a life that means something. That's exactly why I do this — every single day I get to help someone, and I'm becoming the kind of man I want to be. That "good life" you want for me? I'm already on that road.

Closer You don't have to agree with me today. Just don't worry ahead of me. Give me a bit of time — and let the results do the talking.

两个领袖意见相左 · 调解开场Two Leaders Clash · Mediation

礼物思维 + 甜咸比 · 先降温，再对焦Gift Mindset + 4:1 · Cool it down, then refocus

先停一下，两位都听我一句。这件事会吵，正正说明你们两个都太上心了——一个不在乎的人，是不会为了做法争的。这点我很感激。

我听下来，A 你要的是快、别错过时机；B 你要的是稳、别把人做伤。你们要的其实不冲突，你们都在护同一个团队。

所以我们不争「谁对」，我们只问一句：哪个做法，能让团队又快又不伤人？来，各讲一个你的方案，我们挑出两边都能接受的那一半，今天就定下来。

Hold on — both of you, hear me out for a second. The fact that this got heated tells me one thing: you both care too much to let it slide. People who don't care never argue about how to do things. And honestly, I'm grateful for that.

Here's what I'm hearing. A, you want speed — don't miss the window. B, you want it steady — don't burn people out. Those two things are not enemies. You're both protecting the same team.

So we're not going to fight about who's right. One question only: which way gets this team moving fast without hurting anyone? Come — one plan from each of you. We take the half that both sides can live with, and we lock it in today.

S1「不过是个推销的」"Just a Salesman"

小故事 → 道理 → 主张 · Story → Lesson → Line

小故事 有一次饭局，有人知道我做什么之后，笑着说：「哦，不过是个跑业务的嘛。」那一桌人都笑了，我也跟着笑，但心里记住了这句话。我没有反驳，我回去把它变成动力。

道理 别人怎么定义你，不重要；你用什么把那个定义撑破，才重要。看轻你的人，反而帮你把目标写清楚了。

主张 所以我从不急着解释我是谁，我用我带出来的人，替我说话。

Story At a dinner once, someone found out what I do and said, with a smile, "Oh — so basically a salesman lah." The whole table laughed. I laughed along. But I kept that sentence, took it home, and turned it into fuel.

Lesson How people define you doesn't matter. What you build to break that definition — that's what matters. The people who look down on you are really just writing your goals down for you.

Line So I never rush to explain who I am. The people I've built — they speak for me.

S2建人，不只建单Build People, Not Just Deals

小故事 → 道理 → 主张 · Story → Lesson → Line

小故事 前几年有个机会，走一条路能快很多，但那条路是把人当数字。我犹豫了很久，最后选了慢的那条——先把人带成人。结果那几个当年我肯花时间的人，今天都能独当一面了。

道理 你把人当数字，人就散；你把人当人，人就跟你走一辈子。慢，有时候才是最快的路。

主张 我宁可慢慢建一个人，也不要快快签一张单。

Story A few years back, there was a shortcut in front of me — a much faster road, but one that treated people as numbers. I sat with it for a long time, and in the end I chose the slow road: build the person first. The ones I invested those hours in? Today they stand on their own two feet.

Lesson Treat people as numbers, and they scatter. Treat people as people, and they'll walk with you for life. Sometimes the slow road is the fastest one there is.

Line I'd rather build one person slowly than close one deal quickly.

S3给孩子示范勇气Show My Kids Courage

小故事 → 道理 → 主张 · Story → Lesson → Line

小故事 有天晚上我要上台，其实很紧张，我家小孩看着我问：「爸爸你怕吗？」我蹲下来跟他说：「怕，但爸爸还是会上去。」那天我上台前一直想着这句话，因为我知道有一双眼睛在学我怎么面对害怕。

道理 勇气不是不怕，是怕，还照样去做。孩子不会照你说的做，他们会照你做的学。

主张 我上的每一次台，都是在给我的孩子示范：怕，也要往前走。

Story One night before I went on stage, I was honestly nervous. My kid looked up at me and asked, "Papa, are you scared?" I knelt down and said, "Yes. But Papa is still going up there." I carried that one sentence with me onto the stage — because I knew a small pair of eyes was learning from me how to face fear.

Lesson Courage is not the absence of fear. It's being afraid — and doing it anyway. Kids don't follow what you say. They copy what you do.

Line Every stage I step onto is a lesson for my kids: be scared, and go anyway.

DAY 2相似性联结 Common Ground

练：想一个「我跟你一样」的真实经历，写成两句话。

Drill: Find one true "I was just like you" moment; write it in two lines.

时机：出门开会前，在车上讲给自己听一次。

When: In the car, before your first meeting — say it once out loud.

完成：我能说出一句「我以前也……」的真实共同点。

Done: I can say one true "I used to be the same" line.

DAY 3甜咸比 4:1 4:1 Ratio

练：挑一个团队领袖，准备四句真心的肯定 + 一句建议。

Drill: Pick one leader: prepare four honest affirmations plus one suggestion.

时机：当天真的找他谈一次，先给四句再给一句。

When: Actually have that talk today — four first, then the one.

完成：我先讲满四句肯定，才放那一句建议。

Done: Four full affirmations land before the one suggestion.

DAY 4化繁为简 Simplify

练：把你今天想教的东西，砍到只剩「一个核心＋三步」。

Drill: Cut today's teaching down to one core idea plus three steps.

时机：午休时写在手机备忘录里，删到不能再删。

When: Lunch break, phone notes — cut until nothing more can go.

完成：我的重点浓缩成一句核心，加三个步骤。

Done: My point fits in one core line plus three steps.

DAY 5礼物思维 Gift Mindset

练：见一个人之前，先问自己：我能送他什么对他有用的？

Drill: Before any meeting, ask: what can I give this person that helps?

时机：任一场约见或咖啡前，心里过一遍这句话。

When: Before any coffee or appointment, run the question once.

完成：这次见面我没急着说服，而是先给出一样东西。

Done: I gave something useful before trying to convince.

DAY 6复盘 Review

练：回看这周的语音笔记，挑出讲得最顺的一段留起来。

Drill: Replay the week's voice notes; keep your smoothest take.

时机：晚饭后十分钟，安静听自己一周的录音。

When: Ten quiet minutes after dinner with the week's recordings.

完成：我选出了本周最好的一段，知道好在哪。

Done: I picked my best take and I know why it works.

DAY 7实战＋家庭 Family Round

练：用「小故事→道理→主张」的架子，讲一件事给家人听。

Drill: Tell your family one story using story–lesson–line.

时机：晚饭桌上，讲一个你自己的小故事给孩子听。

When: At the dinner table — one small story for the kids.

完成：家人听完有回应，我讲完不心虚。

Done: The family responds, and I don't feel fake saying it.

规则只有一条：宁可每天五分钟，也别一周憋一次大的。开口是肌肉，天天动才长。

One rule: five minutes daily beats one big push a week. Speaking is a muscle — it grows when you move it every day.
