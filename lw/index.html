M1Light Warriors 是什么（60 秒）What Is Light Warriors (60 sec)

反差钩子 + 化繁为简 · 身份层面的分享，不谈任何产品Contrast Hook + Simplify · identity-level sharing, zero product talk

很多人问我：「光之战士？听起来是不是很夸张？」老实说，我第一次听也是这样想的。

其实我们就是一群很平凡的人——爸爸妈妈、上班族、做小生意的。平凡到有一天我们发现，家人最需要我们的时候，我们自己却先累垮了。

所以我们决定从自己开始：先把健康顾好，再让自己成长，成长到有能力了，就回头带身边的人一起走。健康、成长、领导力，这个顺序不能倒。

我们的信念很简单：建立事业、建立人、建立领袖、建立传承。Soul + System = Success——用心做人，用系统做事。

你不需要很厉害才开始。你只要愿意，先成为你家里的那盏灯。

A lot of people ask me, "Light Warriors? Isn't that a bit dramatic?" Honestly, that was my first reaction too.

But really, we're just a group of very ordinary people: parents, office workers, small business owners. So ordinary that one day we realised: the moment our families needed us most, we were the ones running on empty.

So we decided to start with ourselves. Get our health right first. Then grow as a person. And once we've grown enough to carry others, we turn back and bring the people around us along. Wellness, growth, leadership. The order cannot be reversed.

Our creed is simple: build the business, build people, build leaders, build a legacy. Soul + System = Success: heart in the person, system in the work.

You don't have to be great to start. You just have to be willing — to be the first light in your own home.

M2领袖个人故事框架 + 示范Your Personal Story Frame + Example

小故事 → 道理 → 主张 · 换成你自己的真实小事Story → Lesson → Line · swap in your own true moment

框架：一件真实的小事（三四句，有画面）→ 它教会我的一句道理 → 一句主张，讲完就停。

Frame: One true small moment (3–4 lines, make it visual) → the one lesson it taught you → one line of conviction. Then stop.

小故事 我们团队有一位伙伴，刚来的时候连自我介绍都不敢，轮到她就低头。半年后的一个晚上，我看到她站在自己家的客厅，给七八个亲戚朋友做分享。讲完她走过来跟我说：「原来我可以。」

道理 人不是先有信心才开口，是先开口，信心才慢慢长出来。

主张 所以我带人，从来不等他「准备好」，我陪他先开第一次口。

Story One of our partners, when she first joined, didn't even dare do a self-intro. Her turn comes, she looks at the floor. Six months later, one evening, I watched her stand in her own living room, sharing with seven or eight relatives and friends. Afterwards she came over and said, "So I actually can."

Lesson Confidence doesn't come before you speak. You speak first, and confidence slowly grows from there.

Line So when I build people, I never wait for them to feel "ready." I walk with them through that first time they open their mouth.

I1邀朋友来分享会Invite a Friend to a Sharing

礼物思维 · 零压力 · 「合不合适你自己判断」Gift Mindset · zero pressure · "you be the judge"

最近我在参与一个我自己觉得很不错的社群。这个星期有一场分享，讲的是怎么把健康和个人成长这两件事，一起做起来。

我第一个就想到你——不是要你做什么，是我觉得里面有几样东西，你听了应该会有共鸣。

就一个多小时，你来听听看。合不合适你，你自己判断，怎样我都 OK。

Recently I've been part of a community that I honestly really like. This week there's a sharing session. It's about getting your health and your personal growth moving at the same time.

You were the first person I thought of. Not because I need you to do anything. I just think a few things in there will really speak to you.

It's just over an hour. Come and have a listen lah. Whether it fits you or not, you be the judge. Either way I'm totally fine.

I2邀约健康体验活动Invite to a Wellness Experience

相似性联结 + 礼物思维 · 从对方讲过的话切入Common Ground + Gift · start from what they told you

你上次不是说，最近老是觉得累，想调整生活习惯吗？我们这个周末刚好有一个健康体验活动，很轻松的，学一些照顾身体的基本功，现场也可以自己感受一下。

我不敢说它一定适合你。但去体验一下，没有什么损失。

你来感受，觉得有用就继续了解，觉得不适合，就当多认识几个朋友。真的没压力。

Remember you told me you've been feeling tired all the time, and you wanted to sort out your lifestyle? This weekend we happen to have a wellness experience event, very relaxed one. You learn some basics of taking care of your body, and you can feel it for yourself on the spot.

I won't claim it's confirmed right for you. But going to experience it — nothing to lose, right?

Come and feel it. If it's useful, find out more. If it's not for you, then you've made a few new friends. No pressure, really.

I3初次接触后的跟进Follow-Up After First Contact

48 小时内 · 给予而非征服Within 48 hours · give, don't conquer

嗨，是我。那天跟你聊得很开心，我一直记得你说，想在下班后找一件让自己进步的事。

我回去想了想，把那天提到的那场分享的时间发给你。你有空就来，没空也完全没关系。

另外，我找到一篇我自己很受用的东西，先发给你——这个是送你的，跟你来不来没有关系。

Hey, it's me. I really enjoyed our chat that day. I keep thinking about what you said, that you want something after work that helps you grow.

I went back and thought about it, so here's the date and time for that sharing we talked about. Come if you're free; if you can't make it, no issue at all.

Also, I found something that helped me a lot personally, and I'm sending it to you first. That one is a gift. It's yours whether you come or not.

W1我的健康之旅（60 秒）My Wellness Journey (60 sec)

相似性联结 · 只讲自己的旅程，不讲产品Common Ground · your journey only, no product talk

我跟很多人一样，以前觉得年轻就是本钱——熬夜、乱吃，身体永远排在最后一位。直到有一段时间，我每天醒来都觉得没电，那次真的吓到我。

后来我开始认真学怎么照顾自己：了解营养，学一些细胞健康的基本概念，然后把睡眠、喝水、运动、营养，一样一样补回来。

这一路走下来，我自己的精神和状态好了很多。当然，每个人的身体不一样，效果也不同——这个只是我自己的经历。

如果你也想开始，不用一次改变全部。先从一个小习惯开始，就够了。

I was like a lot of people: I thought being young was capital enough. Late nights, eating anyhow, body always last on the list. Then came a stretch where I woke up flat every single day. That one really scared me.

So I started properly learning how to look after myself: understanding nutrition, picking up the basics of cellular health, and rebuilding one thing at a time: sleep, water, movement, nutrition.

Along the way, my own energy and overall condition improved a lot. Of course, everybody's body is different and results vary. This is just my own journey.

If you want to start too, don't change everything at once. One small habit first. That's enough.

W2朋友问：「这些东西有什么用？」When a Friend Asks: "What's It Actually For?"

化繁为简 · 合规用语：有助于支持／促进／维持／辅助Simplify · compliant verbs: supports / promotes / maintains / assists

好问题。我不讲夸张的，我只讲两件事。

第一，根本永远是生活方式——好的营养、睡眠和运动，有助于支持身体的正常运作，也有助于维持你每天的状态。这个是地基，谁都绕不过。

第二，我自己的体验是，把这些基本功做扎实之后，整个人的精神好了很多。但我也要老实讲：每个人的效果不同，你要自己体验过，才知道适不适合你。

所以我不说服你，我只邀请你了解。要不要继续，你自己判断。

Good question. I don't do exaggerated talk. Let me just say two things.

First, the foundation is always lifestyle. Good nutrition, sleep and movement help support the way your body normally functions, and help maintain your day-to-day condition. That's the base. Nobody gets around it.

Second, from my own experience: once I got these basics solid, my energy really came up. But let me be honest with you: results vary from person to person. You have to experience it yourself to know whether it suits you.

So I won't persuade you. I'll just invite you to find out. Whether you take it further, that's your call.

五个技巧 · 教给你的伙伴The 5 Moves · Coach Your Team With These

① 礼物思维：出门前先问一句——「我今天能送对方什么有用的？」

① Gift Mindset: before you go, ask: "what useful thing can I give this person today?"

② 相似性联结：开口先讲「我跟你一样」，再讲道理。

② Common Ground: open with "I was just like you" before any lesson.

③ 甜咸比 4:1：纠偏之前，先给足四句真心的肯定。

③ 4:1 Ratio: four honest affirmations land before the one correction.

④ 反差钩子：先替对方说出他心里的怀疑，再翻转。

④ Contrast Hook: say their doubt out loud for them first, then flip it.

⑤ 化繁为简：一个核心，三个步骤，其余的删掉。

⑤ Simplify: one core, three steps. Cut everything else.

甜咸比 4:1 框架The 4:1 Frame

肯定 ×4（要具体、要真，不可以敷衍）→ 建议 ×1（要小、当天就能做）→ 收尾一句：「我陪你。」

Affirm ×4 (specific and true, no empty praise) → Suggest ×1 (small, doable today) → Close with one line: "I'll walk it with you."

教伙伴时的口诀：先把人肯定到站稳了，再动他一个小地方。

Coaching cue: steady the person with affirmation first, then move one small thing.

小故事 → 道理 → 主张Story → Lesson → Line

一件真实的小事（三四句，有画面）→ 一句道理 → 一句主张。讲完就停，不要加料。

One true small moment (3–4 lines, visual) → one lesson → one line. Then stop. Don't pad it.

教伙伴时的口诀：故事要小到像昨天发生的，主张要短到一口气讲完。

Coaching cue: the story should feel like it happened yesterday; the line should fit in one breath.
